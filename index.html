About Us

Who We Are

Due to the effects of the Covid-19 panademic, more people are experiencing food insecurity. Food Banks are providing food to individuals, families, and seniors. Our goal is to help you find food, volunteer, or make a charitable donation.

Our Mission

Assist individuals in finding food, whether it's groceries for the week, or a hot meal. We would like to strengthen our community by connecting individuals looking to volunteer with a local hunger relief organization, and individuals with resources to make a financial contributon.

How We Help

Philadelphia is one of the many cities across America that has been hit hard with food insecurity. We will connect you with a local food bank, soup kitchen, or organizations that can assist you. Let's work together to help provide hunger relief.
