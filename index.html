About Us

Who We Are

Feeding Philly is a non-profit organization leading the movement to end hunger in the City of Brotherly Love. We supply the food to hundreds of food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and faith-based organizations.

Our Mission

Feeding people, strengthening communities, and ending hunger for more Philadelpians. We supplement this work with outreach programs that provide direct food assistance, educate the public on the importance of good nutrition.

How We Help

Today, we are serving more working families, more children, and more seniors. We need to offer solutions that help Philadelphia communities end hunger for good.
